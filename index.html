Joohee Lee   |   CV

I'm an Assistant Professor in the Department of Convergence Security Engineering at the Sungshin Women's University. Prior to joining Sungshin Women's University, I was with the Security Algorithm Team at Samsung SDS from 2019 to 2022. Before that, I received a Ph.D. degree in Mathematical Sciences at Seoul National University(SNU) under the supervision of Prof. Jung Hee Cheon in 2019, and I received a B.S. degree in the Department of Mathematics Education at Korea University in 2013.
I'm leading the SCRYPT (Sungshin CRYPTography) LAB. Our research group studies Lattice-based cryptography, Post-quantum cryptography, Cryptographic protocols, and Privacy-enhancing techniques.
